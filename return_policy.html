Return Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

We offer refund/exchange within the first 1 day from the date of your purchase. If 1 day has passed since your purchase, you will not be offered a return, exchange or refund of any kind.

Eligibility for Return or Exchange

To become eligible for a return or an exchange:

  • The purchased item should be unused and in the same condition as you received it;
  • The item must have original packaging;
  • If the item was purchased on sale, it may not be eligible for a return/exchange.

Only items found defective or damaged will be replaced (based on an exchange request) after inspection.

Exempted Categories

There may be certain categories of products/items that are exempted from returns or refunds. Such categories will be identified to you at the time of purchase.

Return / Exchange Process

For accepted return/exchange requests, once your returned product/item is received and inspected by us, we will send you an email to notify you about receipt of the returned/exchanged product. If the return/exchange is approved after our quality check, your request will be processed in accordance with our policies.

Contact Customer Service

Please contact our customer service team via the contact details on the website and provide your order details and a description of the issue to help us process your request quickly.
