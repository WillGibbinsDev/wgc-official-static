Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

This refund and cancellation policy outlines how you can cancel or seek a refund for a product or service that you have purchased through the Platform.

Cancellation Window

Cancellations will only be considered if the request is made within 1 day of placing the order. However, cancellation requests may not be entertained if the orders have already been communicated to the sellers/merchants listed on the Platform and they have initiated the shipping process, or the product is out for delivery. In such an event, you may choose to reject the product at the doorstep.

9778226340 does not accept cancellation requests for perishable items like flowers, eatables, etc. However, refund or replacement can be considered if the user establishes that the quality of the product delivered is not satisfactory.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive damaged or defective items, please report this to our customer service team. The request will be entertained once the seller/merchant listed on the Platform has checked and determined the issue at its end. This should be reported within 1 day of receipt of the products.

Items Not as Described

If you feel that the product received is not as shown on the site or not as per your expectations, you must notify our customer service within 1 day of receiving the product. Our customer service team will review the complaint and take an appropriate decision.

Manufacturer Warranty

For products that come with a manufacturer's warranty, please refer the issue directly to the manufacturer as per the warranty terms.

Refund Processing

In case any refunds are approved by 9778226340, it will take 1 day for the refund to be processed to you. The actual time for funds to reflect in your account may vary depending on your payment provider or bank.

Contact Customer Service

Please contact our customer service team through the contact details on the website. Provide your order details and a description of the issue to help us process your request quickly.
